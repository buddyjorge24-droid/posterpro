About NMG Construction

Phoenix's trusted general contractor — built on a foundation of integrity, quality, and exceptional service.

Our Story

Founded in Phoenix, NMG Construction was built with a simple mission: provide high-quality general contracting services across the Valley of the Sun without the headaches that too often come with construction projects. We've built our reputation project by project — through transparent communication, fair pricing, and uncompromising craftsmanship.

What sets us apart is our structured approach. We don't just take on any project that comes our way. We carefully vet every lead to ensure we can deliver the quality our clients expect, and we're honest when a project isn't the right fit for us. This means when we say yes, you can count on us to deliver.

Today, we serve homeowners, property managers, and commercial clients across Phoenix, Scottsdale, Mesa, Chandler, Gilbert, and Tempe, with a loyal base of repeat customers who refer us to their friends, family, and colleagues throughout the Valley.

Our Process

From initial consultation to final walkthrough, we keep you informed and involved.

1

Free Consultation

Tell us about your project. We'll discuss your vision, budget, and timeline to see if we're a good fit. This can be done over the phone, via video call, or in person.

2

Detailed Estimate

We provide a comprehensive written estimate with clear line items for materials, labor, and any subcontractor work. No fine print, no hidden fees.

3

Planning & Permits

Once you approve the estimate, we handle all planning, material ordering, and permit applications so you don't have to worry about the details.

4

Construction

Our crew gets to work with regular updates and scheduled walkthroughs. You'll always know where we are in the process and what to expect next.

5

Final Walkthrough

We review every detail together. If anything isn't perfect, we make it right on the spot. We don't consider a project done until you're completely satisfied.
